FAQ

Questions, answered.

Can't find what you're looking for? Email us.

Do I need to create an account?

Nope. HabitAI works fully offline with no sign-up. You can optionally sign in with Google to enable encrypted cloud sync across devices.

Does the AI assistant see my habits?

The voice and chat assistant only sends the minimum context it needs to understand your command. You can disable AI features entirely in Settings.

Will I lose my streak if I miss a day?

No, streak math respects your schedule. Skip a day on a daily habit and we'll suggest either catching up or rolling forward, never silently resetting you.

What's included for free?

Unlimited habits, every habit type, every schedule, streaks, history, basic analytics, all themes, and the AI assistant. Optional cloud sync and exports are available as in-app upgrades.

Can I export my data?

Yes. One-tap export of your full habit history is available as an in-app upgrade. You can always request a copy by emailing us.
