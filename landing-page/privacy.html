1. Introduction

HabitAI ("we", "our", "the app") is built on a fundamental belief: your personal data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the HabitAI mobile application and related services.

By using HabitAI, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary for the app to function. We do not collect or sell your personal information for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data you provide directly

  • Habit data: Names, targets, schedules, notes, completion logs, and streak history you create.
  • App preferences: Theme selection, notification settings, and other configuration choices.
  • Voice commands: Audio recordings are processed on-device where possible. When processed by our AI assistant, transcripts are sent temporarily and not stored long-term.
  • Google account information: If you enable cloud sync, we receive your Google profile name and email address solely for authentication.

Data collected automatically

  • Crash reports: Anonymous crash data to help us fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Usage metrics: Anonymous feature usage statistics (e.g., number of habits created, AI queries made) with no personally identifying information. You may opt out at any time in Settings.

We do not collect or store your IP address, device identifiers, location data, contact list, or browsing history.

3. Local-First Architecture

HabitAI is designed as a local-first application. By default, all your data — habits, logs, preferences, streak history — is stored exclusively on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

This means:

  • You can use HabitAI fully offline with no account.
  • Your data stays on your device and is not accessible to us.
  • No network requests are made for core app functionality.
  • You control if and when your data leaves your device.

4. AI Assistant & Data Processing

HabitAI includes an optional AI assistant that helps you create, modify, and log habits using natural language.

How it works

  • When you speak or type a command, the app sends only the minimum context required to understand your intent.
  • Transcripts are processed in real time and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • The AI does not have access to your full habit history unless needed to fulfill your request.

Your control

  • You may disable AI features entirely in Settings at any time.
  • With AI disabled, all habit management works without any network communication.
  • You may request deletion of any AI processing logs by contacting us.

5. Cloud Sync & Account Data

Cloud sync is an optional feature. When enabled, we use Google Sign-In for authentication. The following data is synced to our servers and encrypted at rest:

  • Habit definitions and schedules
  • Completion logs and streak data
  • App preferences and settings

Encryption

All sync data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256. We employ end-to-end encryption for sensitive habit data, ensuring that even we cannot read your habit content.

Deleting synced data

You may delete your synced data at any time from within the app. Doing so does not affect data stored locally on your device.

6. Third-Party Services

HabitAI integrates with the following third-party services, all optional:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google Sign-In Authentication for cloud sync Profile name, email address
AI processing provider Natural language command parsing Command transcript (not stored)
Crash reporting (optional) Anonymous crash analytics Crash stack trace, device type (no identifiers)

We do not use analytics services like Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising SDKs. No data is shared with data brokers.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Local data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or manually clear data. You can export and delete your data at any time from within the app.

Cloud sync data: Retained until you delete it from the app or request account deletion. We will delete all server data within 30 days of receiving a deletion request.

Usage metrics: Aggregated and anonymized. Retained for up to 12 months for product improvement purposes.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to deletion: Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing of your personal data.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@habitai.in. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app (e.g., a notice in the app or an email if you have cloud sync enabled). We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of HabitAI after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
